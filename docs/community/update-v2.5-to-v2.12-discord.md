# Discord post — ShatterApp update v2.5 → v2.12

**ShatterApp — update roundup (v2.5 → v2.12)**

Hey all — a lot has landed on [shatterapp.com](https://shatterapp.com) recently, so here's what's new:

**⚙️ Settings panel**
Gear icon in the nav. Auto-mark units owned, track painted/based status per model, toggle which tabs appear in Play and Roll.

**🏆 Longshanks export**
Build tab → export your list as an SPT code. Paste directly into longshanks.org for tournament registration.

**🃏 Threemiere format**
Three-squad format now sits alongside Standard and Premiere in the Build tab. Old lists migrate automatically.

**🎲 Random Strike Force**
Fill your empty squad slots instantly with a faction-aware random pick. Toggle "Owned" to restrict it to your collection.

**📲 QR code sharing**
Tap the QR icon on any saved list and share the scannable code. No need to copy/paste links.

**🖼️ List visualiser**
Render your list as a card-art grid, download it as a PNG, or share it with a preview image. Pick which squads to show on Premiere/Threemiere lists.

**🔍 Advanced Browse filters**
Filter units by PC range, Force value, Stamina, and Durability. Useful for narrowing down picks for a specific slot.

**🃏 Order Deck (Play tab)**
Standard and Key Operations mode now has a full order deck builder inside Play — build from your roster, shuffle, draw, discard.

**📊 Probability Calculator (Roll tab)**
New tab in Roll. Pick your attack and defence dice and see cumulative hit probabilities across 50,000 simulated rolls. Enable it in Settings first.
