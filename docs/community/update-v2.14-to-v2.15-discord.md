# Discord post — ShatterApp update v2.14 → v2.15

**ShatterApp v2.15 — Morgan Elsbeth + mobile builder fixes**

Hey all — a couple of updates just landed on [shatterapp.com](https://shatterapp.com):

---

**🗡️ New unit — Morgan Elsbeth (v2.15)**

Morgan Elsbeth from **SWP83: My Loyalty, My Life** is now in the app. Front card, ability card, and both stance cards are in. Order card will be added when available.

Her abilities are extracted and searchable:
- **Fate Has Decided Our Next Move** — manipulate your Order Deck at the start of activation for movement or allied Force recovery
- **Manipulating Hand** — force an enemy character to move; you resolve the move
- **You Will Die Here, Alone** — bonus dice on melee attacks when the defender has no nearby allies
- **My Loyalty, My Life for the Sisterhood** — absorb allied Force costs while healthy; trigger a 5-dice melee counter when wounded

**📅 Last Updated dates (v2.15)**
Unit profiles now show a **Last Updated** date instead of the old card art status badge. Every profile has a date — new additions are stamped on release day.

---

**📱 Custom Builder — mobile overhaul (v2.14)**

22 mobile UX fixes landed across all four builder phases. The big ones:

- Combat tree icons can now be repositioned on touch devices (long-press + tap)
- Expertise delete badge is always visible — no hover required
- Canvas previews are crisp on retina displays
- All touch targets raised to 44px minimum
- Large photo uploads compressed before storing
- ProfileVisualModal cards stack vertically on mobile; PDF export is first in the action list
