# Discord post — ShatterApp update v2.11 → v2.13

**ShatterApp v2.13 — Custom Profiles + a few other updates**

Hey all — here's what's landed on [shatterapp.com](https://shatterapp.com) since the last update:

---

**🛠️ Custom Profiles (v2.13 — headline feature)**

The new **Custom** tab lets you build your own homebrew unit profiles from the ground up. Every part of the card is covered:

**Front card** — unit type, cost, FP, era, faction, portrait upload. Renders live as you fill it in.

**Stats & Abilities** — Stamina, Durability, tags, and a full ability block editor (Tactic / Active / Reactive / Innate / Identity with Force costs). An **insights panel** shows percentile bands so you can see how your unit compares to the official roster.

**Stances** — ranged and melee attack/defence dice, range, weapons, equipment, expertise table, and combat tree. Same percentile insights for every combat stat.

**Order card** — configure whether the unit has a Tactic ability, with a live card preview.

Hit **Publish** and the unit appears in your Build roster like any official unit. The preview modal shows all four cards side by side and lets you download them as a PDF.

This is the biggest single feature drop so far — there's more to build on top of it, but it's fully usable now. Let me know what you think.

---

**🖼️ List visualiser (v2.11)**
Render your list as a card-art grid, download as PNG, or share with a social preview link. Premiere/Threemiere lists let you pick which squads to show.

**📊 Probability Calculator (v2.12)**
New tab in Roll — pick your dice and see cumulative hit probabilities across 50k simulated rolls. Enable in Settings.

**❤️ Play wound flow rework (v2.12.1)**
Filling the damage track now sets a Wounded condition rather than auto-triggering. Flip Wounded clears damage and adds an Injured dot. Renamed "Wounds" → "Injured" throughout.

---
